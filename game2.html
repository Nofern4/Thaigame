2. Ree Ree Khao San

Ree Ree Khao San involves two players forming an arch with their arms. Other players form a line holding waists and walk through the arch while singing the song.

When the song ends, the arch comes down to catch the last person in line. The caught player usually has to leave the game or accept a small penalty. It's great for rhythm and teamwork.
